About This System

What is the Dietary Recommendation System?

This system uses machine learning (specifically K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm) to provide personalized dietary recommendations based on your health profile. By analyzing your health metrics and comparing them with similar user profiles in our database, we can suggest the most suitable diet for your needs.

How Does It Work?
  1. Input Your Information: You provide your age, weight, height, activity level, health conditions, and dietary preferences.
  2. Feature Extraction: The system converts your information into numerical features (BMI, activity factor, health severity, etc.).
  3. KNN Algorithm: We find the K-Nearest Neighbors (similar users) in our database using Euclidean distance in the feature space.
  4. Recommendation: Based on the recommended diets of similar users, we suggest the most suitable diet options for you.
What is the K-Nearest Neighbors Algorithm?

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) is a simple yet powerful machine learning algorithm that:

  • Stores all training data and makes predictions based on similarity
  • For a new user, finds the K closest users in the training data
  • Uses the characteristics of those K similar users to make recommendations
  • Is non-parametric and lazy learner (doesn't build an explicit model)
